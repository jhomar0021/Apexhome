Our Legacy

Building Foundations Since 2006

Apex Homes started with a simple vision: to eliminate the stress from custom home building while delivering architectural integrity. What began as a two-man contracting crew has grown into one of the region's most trusted full-service building companies.

Over the last two decades, we've successfully handed over keys to hundreds of families. Our milestone metrics aren't measured just in square footage, but in the generational trust our clients place in our craft.
